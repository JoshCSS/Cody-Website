The 7th Sky Corps

The 7th Sky Corps was a military corps of the Grand Army of the Republic. Like other units in the Grand Army, the ranks of the 7th consisted of clone troopers that had been engineered to fight for the Galactic Republic. During the Clone Wars, the 7th was led by Jedi General Obi-Wan Kenobi and Marshal Commander CC-2224 "Cody." As a corps within the Grand Army of the Republic, the 7th Sky Corps consisted of 36,864 troopers divided into sixteen regiments.

As a corps within the Grand Army of the Republic, the 7th Sky Corps consisted of 36,864 troopers divided into sixteen regiments. The corps also contained multiple battalions, including the 212th Attack Battalion. The 212th Recon Division was a unit under the 212th Attack Battalion. 256 companies were part of the 7th Sky Corps, including the 212th's Ghost Company. Waxer's platoon in the 212th was one of 1,024 platoons that made up the 7th Sky Corps, and a total of 4,096 squads made up the corps. The 7th Sky Corps also contained Foxtrot Group, a unit of special forces led by Clone Captain CC-5576-39 "Gregor." The 7th Sky Corps was under the overall command of High Jedi General Obi-Wan Kenobi and his clone trooper officer, Clone Marshal Commander CC-2224 "Cody," with the duo personally commanding the 212th Attack Battalion.

Troop Types: Sky Corps

The standard clone troopers of the 7th Sky Corps were identified by their color scheme of orange on white clone trooper armor, and ARF troopers of the corps used orange and brown camouflage. At least one member, "Crys," customized his armor with yellow on his white armor, instead of the standard orange.

The 7th Sky Corps utilized various vehicles from its arsenal. Its ground components utilized All Terrain Reconnaissance Transports, All Terrain Tactical Enforcers, and BARC speeders. Aerial components used LAAT/i gunships and LAAT carriers. Members of the 7th Sky Corps were at times stationed on Venator-class Star Destroyers such as the Negotiator, as well as Arquitens-class light cruisers.
